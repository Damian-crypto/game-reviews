First Person Shooter - FPS

A first-person shooter (FPS) is a type of video game genre that emphasizes fast-paced action and focuses on the player's perspective. The player typically controls a character or avatar who must navigate through various levels or environments while shooting or otherwise eliminating enemy characters. The action is viewed from a first-person perspective, meaning that the player sees the world through the eyes of their character.

Massively Multiplayer Online - MMO

A massively multiplayer online game (MMOG) is a type of online game that allows thousands of players to participate and play together in a virtual world. Unlike traditional online games, which usually support a limited number of players, MMOGs support large numbers of simultaneous players in a shared game world.

Multiplayer Online Battle Arena - MOBA

A multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) is a type of online video game that typically features two teams of players battling each other in an arena-style environment. Each player controls a single character, or "hero," with unique abilities, and works with their team to destroy the enemy team's base while defending their own. MOBAs are known for their strategic depth and require coordination and communication among team members to achieve victory.

Real Time Strategy - RTS

Real-time strategy games (RTS) are a genre of video games where players control armies or factions in real-time, using a combination of resource management, base building, and tactical combat to achieve their objectives. Players typically start with a small base and must gather resources (such as minerals, food, or gold) to build structures and train units. They can then use these units to explore the game world, attack enemies, and defend their own base. In real-time strategy games, the action happens in real-time, which means that players must make quick decisions and respond rapidly to changing situations on the battlefield. Real-time strategy games can be played against the computer (in a single-player campaign or skirmish mode) or against other human players (in multiplayer matches).

Far Cry 1

The story of Far Cry 1 follows Jack Carver, a former Special Forces operative who has become a freelance boat charterer. He is hired by a journalist named Valerie Cortez to take her to an island where she believes she will find evidence of illegal activity by a scientist named Dr. Krieger. However, when they arrive on the island, they are attacked by mercenaries who are under the command of Dr. Krieger. Jack manages to escape and teams up with an ally named Doyle to rescue Valerie and uncover the truth behind Dr. Krieger's experiments. Throughout the game, Jack must fight his way through hordes of mercenaries and mutants that were created as a result of Dr. Krieger's experiments. Along the way, he uncovers a larger conspiracy involving a mysterious organization known as "The Cartel" and their plans for world domination. Far Cry 1 received critical acclaim for its open-world gameplay, stunning graphics, and engaging storyline. It went on to become one of the most successful video game franchises of all time, spawning several sequels and spin-off games.

Far Cry 2

Set in a fictional Central African country engulfed in civil war, the storyline follows a mercenary who is assigned to kill the Jackal, a weapons dealer inflaming the conflict. The player navigates the open world, completing missions for factions and allies called Buddies while managing their health and equipment.

FIFA 23 | eChampions League

"eChampions League" which is a tournament organized by EA Sports as part of the FIFA 19 Global Series. It is a competitive tournament for professional FIFA gamers who compete for a chance to win a portion of the prize pool and qualify for the FIFA eWorld Cup. The tournament features some of the best FIFA gamers in the world and is played on the PlayStation 4 console. The eChampions League is separate from the regular UEFA Champions League, which is a real-life football competition.

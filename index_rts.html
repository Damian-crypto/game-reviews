Taur

"Taur" is an engaging and challenging tower defense game that offers a fresh take on the genre with its first-person perspective and strategic depth. In the game, the player takes on the role of a hero who must defend a planet from waves of invading robots by strategically placing towers and using special abilities. The game has received positive reviews from critics and players alike, particularly for its unique gameplay mechanics and stylish graphics.

Age of Empires

"Age of Empires" is a real-time strategy game series that takes place throughout different eras of human history, from ancient civilizations to the colonial period. The game's storyline varies depending on the era and campaign chosen, but generally, the player takes on the role of a leader who must build and manage a civilization, gather resources, and lead their armies to victory.
Throughout the series, players must research technologies, train units, and manage resources such as food, wood, and gold. The game's focus on historical accuracy and attention to detail, such as accurately representing the architecture and units of each civilization, has made it a popular choice for history buffs and gamers alike.
